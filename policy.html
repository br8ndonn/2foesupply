Store Policies

The Fine Print

Last updated June 2026
All Sales Are Final

Every purchase from 2FOESUPPLY is final. We do not offer refunds, returns, exchanges, or store credit on any item once a sale is completed.

No Refunds or Returns

Because every piece is a one-off resale item, all sales are final. Once a transaction is complete, the item is yours — we cannot accept returns, issue refunds, or offer exchanges for any reason, including fit, sizing, or change of mind.

Please ask every question, and request any additional photos or measurements you need, before you buy. We're happy to help you decide — just reach out first.

Item Condition & Description

Items are described as accurately as possible. Many pieces are pre-owned or open-box and may show normal signs of wear consistent with resale. When possible, we recommend inspecting the item in person at the swap meet before purchasing.

Authenticity

We source and sell our pieces as authentic to the best of our knowledge. If you ever have a concern about an item, message us directly and we'll talk it through.

Pricing & Payment

Prices are as listed or as agreed directly over DM. Payment details are arranged in person or through direct message. Items are not held or reserved without prior agreement.

Pickup & Meetups

Most sales happen in person at the swap meet on Saturdays, 7am–3pm. For any meetup, please choose a safe, public location. Shipping, if offered, is arranged case-by-case over DM and is at the buyer's risk once handed off to the carrier.

Sizing & Fit

Sizing is provided where available, but resale fits can vary. Since all sales are final, please confirm measurements and sizing before you commit.

Questions

Reach out before you buy and we'll sort it out: DM @2foe.resells or email br8ndonn.le87@gmail.com.
